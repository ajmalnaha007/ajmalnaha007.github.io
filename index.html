About Us

We are a start-up incubated under IIM LIVE- KOZHIKODE which operates an Internet-based campus TV network named “CT Network” across different Colleges & Universities in Kerala. We aim to provide student-oriented programs via CTN screens to students kept in the college campus canteens which are centrally controlled and monitored. We have selected Canteens as our prime medium for communication as this is the place where students often relax with a cool mind to enjoy their food; and it is here that any mode of communication has a greater advantage of being keenly watched & absorbed by the audience i.e right information at the right time.

Who we are

In this era of rapid technological evolution and with the objective of providing various useful information’s to students i.e right info at right time. CT Network is an example of how the technological changes can be used to help students updated while in campus. Today, our service is available in 51 colleges and 3 universities spanning across 11 districts of Kerala with a total viewership of two (2) lakh+ students and staffs and various college events like FEST, Seminars or any other college events, various Govt. initiatives, Govt. awareness campaigns, Fresher’s Jobs & careers, Start-up kits & events, Scholarship Info. Etc. will be aired through CT Network and students can reap benefits out of this information. CT Network is the best medium were different organizations can interact with such a large student community who are the future of India. Our goal is to bring the best & only useful information to students directly and this make us stand apart & unique from other sources of information.
